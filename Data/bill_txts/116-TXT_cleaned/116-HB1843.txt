A BILL 
To amend the National Emergencies Act to provide that 
a national emergency declared by the President termi-
nates 60 days after the President reprograms or trans-
fers an amount in excess of $250,000,000 for use in 
response to such emergency, unless a joint resolution 
affirming such declaration is enacted into law.
