A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
treat the sharing of nonpublic campaign material by 
a candidate or political committee with a foreign power 
as the solicitation of a contribution or donation in sup-
port of the candidate or committee from a foreign na-
tional for purposes of the ban on such solicitations under 
such Act, and for other purposes.
