A BILL 
To express the sense of Congress that section 502 of the 
National Security Act of 1947, together with other intel-
ligence community authorities, obligate an element of 
the intelligence community to submit to the congressional 
intelligence committees written notification, by not later 
than 7 days after becoming aware, that an individual 
in the executive branch has disclosed covered classified 
information to an official of an adversary foreign govern-
ment using methods other than established intelligence 
channels, and for other purposes.
