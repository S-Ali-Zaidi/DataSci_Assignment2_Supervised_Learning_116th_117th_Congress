RESOLUTION 
Honoring the World Food Program on the occasion of being 
awarded the 2020 Nobel Peace Prize. 
Whereas, on October 9, 2020, the Norwegian Nobel Com-
mittee announced that the Nobel Peace Prize for 2020 
has been awarded to the United Nations World Food 
Program (WFP) ‘‘for its efforts to combat hunger, for its 
contribution to bettering conditions for peace in conflict- 
affected areas and for acting as a driving force in efforts 
to prevent the use of hunger as a weapon of war and con-
flict’’; 
Whereas the WFP is the world’s largest humanitarian orga-
nization addressing hunger and promoting food security;
