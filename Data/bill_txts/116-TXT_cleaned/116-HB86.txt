A BILL 
To provide that the salaries of Members of a House of 
Congress will be held in escrow if that House has not 
agreed to a concurrent resolution on the budget for fiscal 
year 2020 by April 15, 2019, and to withhold the salary 
of the Director of OMB upon failure to submit the 
President’s budget to Congress as required by section 
1105 of title 31, United States Code.
