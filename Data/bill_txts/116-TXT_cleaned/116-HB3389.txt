A BILL 
To require the establishment of a Consumer Price Index 
for Elderly Consumers to compute cost-of-living increases 
for Social Security benefits under title II of the Social 
Security Act and to provide, in the case of elderly bene-
ficiaries under such title, for an annual cost-of-living 
increase which is not less than 3 percent.
