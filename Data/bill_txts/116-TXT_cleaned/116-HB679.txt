A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
apply the restrictions on the use of campaign funds 
for personal use to the funds of leadership PACs and 
other political committees, to clarify the treatment of 
certain coordinated expenditures as contributions to can-
didates, to require the sponsors of certain political adver-
tisements to identify the source of funds used for the 
advertisements, and for other purposes.
