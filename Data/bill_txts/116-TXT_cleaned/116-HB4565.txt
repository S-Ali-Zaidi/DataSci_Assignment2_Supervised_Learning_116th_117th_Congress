A BILL 
To amend the Securities Act of 1933 to require the Securi-
ties and Exchange Commission to issue a rule to apply 
the Administrative Procedure Act to the adoption of ac-
counting principles by the standard setting body, and 
to require the standard setting body to consider, in 
adopting accounting principles, the impact such prin-
ciples will have on the broader U.S. economy, market 
stability, and availability of credit.
