the Sikh, Hindu, and other religious minority commu-
nities in Afghanistan and supporting refugee protection 
for Sikhs and Hindus in Afghanistan in recognition of 
the systematic religious persecution, discrimination, and 
existential danger faced by the members of these commu-
nities.

minorities in Afghanistan, numbering approximately 700
