SEC. 2. PURPOSES. 
4
The purposes of this Act are to— 
5
(1) prohibit predispute arbitration agreements 
6
that require arbitration of work disputes; 
7
(2) prohibit retaliation against workers for re-
8
fusing to arbitrate work disputes; 
9
(3) provide protections to ensure that postdis-
10
pute arbitration agreements are truly voluntary and 
11
with the informed consent of workers; and 
12
(4) amend the National Labor Relations Act to 
13
prohibit agreements and practices that interfere with 
14
employees’ right to engage in concerted activity re-
15
garding work disputes. 
16
