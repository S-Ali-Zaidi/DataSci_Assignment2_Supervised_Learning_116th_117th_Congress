A BILL 
To amend title 31, United States Code, to prohibit the Inter-
nal Revenue Service from carrying out seizures relating 
to a structuring transaction unless the property to be 
seized derived from an illegal source or the funds were 
structured for the purpose of concealing the violation 
of another criminal law or regulation, to require notice 
and a post-seizure hearing for such seizures, and for 
other purposes.
