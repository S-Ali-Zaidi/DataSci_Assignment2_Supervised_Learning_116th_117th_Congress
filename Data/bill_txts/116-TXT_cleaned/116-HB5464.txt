A BILL 
To amend the Congressional Accountability Act of 1995 to 
require Members of Congress to reimburse the Treasury 
for amounts paid as settlements and awards under such 
Act in all cases of employment discrimination acts com-
mitted personally by Members, to require the General 
Counsel of the Office of Congressional Workplace Rights 
to conduct an investigation of all workplace discrimina-
tion claims filed by covered employees under such Act, 
and for other purposes.
