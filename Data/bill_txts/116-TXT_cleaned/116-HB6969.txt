A BILL 
To require that any person that maintains an internet 
website or that sells or distributes a mobile application 
that maintains and stores information collected from 
such website or application in China to disclose that 
such information is stored and maintained in China and 
whether the Chinese Communist Party or a Chinese 
state-owned entity has access to such information.
