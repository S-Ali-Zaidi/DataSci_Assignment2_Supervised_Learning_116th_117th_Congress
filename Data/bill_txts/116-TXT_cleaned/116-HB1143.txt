A BILL 
To amend title XXVII of the Public Health Service Act 
to require a health insurance issuer offering short-term 
limited duration insurance to include a standardized dis-
closure and certain information with respect to coverage 
exclusions and premium variations in marketing, applica-
tion, and enrollment materials distributed in connection 
with such insurance and prohibiting the sale of such 
insurance during certain periods.
