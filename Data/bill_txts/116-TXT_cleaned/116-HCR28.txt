CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for designation of the first Friday of 
October as ‘‘Manufacturing Day’’. 
Whereas according to the most recent data, manufacturers 
contributed $2.18 trillion to the economy, accounting for 
11.2 percent of gross domestic product (GDP); 
Whereas this figure has risen steadily over the past decade, 
from $1.70 trillion in 2009; 
Whereas manufacturers in the United States are a significant 
driver of innovation, spending $211.7 billion on research 
and development in 2016; 
Whereas more than 12.8 million people in the United States 
(or 8 percent of the workforce) are employed directly in 
manufacturing, while many millions more jobs are sup-
ported through the supply chain and related industries;
