A BILL 
To amend the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to require 
individuals applying to register to vote in elections for 
Federal office to provide a complete Social Security num-
ber with the application, to require States to use Social 
Security numbers to cross-check the identifications of 
individuals who vote in elections for Federal office in 
the State with the identifications of individuals who are 
registered to vote in such elections, and for other pur-
poses.
