RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for recognition of June 2019 as National 
Orca Protection Month. 
Whereas orcas are an icon of the Pacific Northwest; 
Whereas orcas are culturally and spiritually important to In-
dian Tribes in the Pacific Northwest; 
Whereas the Puget Sound is home to three pods of Southern 
Resident orcas; 
Whereas the Southern Resident orca population has declined 
dramatically over the past 50 years; 
Whereas the Southern Resident orcas are among the most 
contaminated marine mammals in the world; 
Whereas the Southern Resident orcas are threatened by de-
clining chinook salmon populations;
