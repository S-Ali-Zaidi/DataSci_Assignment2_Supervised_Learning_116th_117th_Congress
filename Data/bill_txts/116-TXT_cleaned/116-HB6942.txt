A BILL 
To require any person that maintains an internet website 
or that sells or distributes a mobile application that 
is owned, wholly or partially, by the Chinese Communist 
Party or by a non-state owned entity located in China, 
to disclose that fact to any individual who downloads 
or otherwise uses such application.
