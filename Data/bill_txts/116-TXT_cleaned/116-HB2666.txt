A BILL 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide 
payment under part A of the Medicare Program on a 
reasonable cost basis for anesthesia services furnished 
by an anesthesiologist in certain rural hospitals in the 
same manner as payments are provided for anesthesia 
services furnished by anesthesiologist assistants and cer-
tified registered nurse anesthetists in such hospitals, and 
for other purposes.
