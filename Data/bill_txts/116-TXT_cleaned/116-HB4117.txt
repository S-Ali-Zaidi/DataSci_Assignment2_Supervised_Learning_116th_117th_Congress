A BILL 
To reduce the disadvantages of individual retirement plans 
with respect to employer-sponsored retirement plans by 
helping taxpayers comply with laws affecting individual 
retirement plans, by providing for reduced penalties 
under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 for certain 
self-corrections with respect to such laws, and by expand-
ing the Employee Plans Compliance Resolution System 
to cover certain errors under individual retirement plans, 
and for other purposes.
