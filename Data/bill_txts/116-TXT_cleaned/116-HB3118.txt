A BILL 
To amend title 18, United States Code, to prohibit former 
Members of Congress from lobbying Congress, to prohibit 
the use of official funds for airline accommodations for 
Members of Congress which are not coach-class accom-
modations, and to prohibit a cost-of-living adjustment 
in the pay rates for Members of Congress during any 
year following a Federal budget deficit.
