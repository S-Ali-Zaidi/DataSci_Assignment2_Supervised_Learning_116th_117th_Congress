A BILL 
To provide that the Federal Communications Commission 
and communications service providers regulated by the 
Commission under the Communications Act of 1934 shall 
not be subject to certain provisions of the National Envi-
ronmental Policy Act of 1969 and the National Historic 
Preservation Act with respect to the construction, re-
building, or hardening of communications facilities fol-
lowing a major disaster or an emergency declared by 
the President, and for other purposes.
