A BILL 
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal 
certain rules related to the determination of unrelated 
business taxable income, to provide that reimbursements 
for costs of using passenger automobiles for charitable 
organizations are excluded from gross income, to make 
the employer credit for paid family and medical leave 
available to tax-exempt eligible employers, and for other 
purposes.
