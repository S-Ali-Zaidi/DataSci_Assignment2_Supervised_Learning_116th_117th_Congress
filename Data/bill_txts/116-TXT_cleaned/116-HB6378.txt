agency regulatory guidance in order to provide forms 
of relief to borrowers and customers during pandemics 
and major disasters, to continue to operate and provide 
services and lending, and to require such regulators to 
conduct testing with financial institutions to assess pan-
demic or disaster preparedness, and for other purposes.
