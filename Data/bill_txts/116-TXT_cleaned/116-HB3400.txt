A BILL 
To amend title 10, United States Code, to authorize the 
enlistment in the Armed Forces of certain aliens who 
are unlawfully present in the United States and were 
younger than 15 years of age when they initially entered 
the United States, but who are otherwise qualified for 
enlistment, and to provide a mechanism by which such 
aliens, by reason of their honorable service in the Armed 
Forces, may be lawfully admitted to the United States 
for permanent residence.
