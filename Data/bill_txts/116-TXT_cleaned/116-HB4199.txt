1
The purpose of this Act is to ensure the public safety 
2
and welfare of communities by promoting evidence-based 
3
approaches that will prevent the tragic toll of gun violence 
4
on families and communities. This purpose can be accom-

plished by—

(1) strengthening and improving intervention 
7

13

(3) restricting gun access by individuals found
