A BILL 
To amend the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to require 
voting systems used in elections for Federal office to 
produce a voter-verified, auditable paper record of the 
votes cast in such elections, to require State election 
officials to audit the results of such elections prior to 
certifying the results, to provide grants to States to 
improve voting system security, and for other purposes.
