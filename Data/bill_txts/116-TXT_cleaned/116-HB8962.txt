A BILL 
To amend the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 to 
prohibit States from registering individuals to vote in 
elections for Federal office who do not provide documen-
tary proof of United States citizenship, to amend the 
Help America Vote Act of 2002 to require individuals 
to provide identification as a condition of voting in elec-
tions for Federal office, and for other purposes.
