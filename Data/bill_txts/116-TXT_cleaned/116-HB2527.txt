A BILL 
To amend the Public Health Service Act to condition receipt 
by States (and political subdivisions and public entities 
of States) of preventive health services grants on the 
establishment of a State requirement for students in 
public elementary and secondary schools to be vaccinated 
in accordance with the recommendations of the Advisory 
Committee on Immunization Practices, and for other 
purposes.
