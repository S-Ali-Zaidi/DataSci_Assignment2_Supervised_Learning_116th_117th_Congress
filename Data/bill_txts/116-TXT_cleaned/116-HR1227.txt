role that whistleblowers play in holding accountable those 
that endanger members of the public and violate this

ees from retaliation for reporting violations of various 
laws related to public health and safety;

well as that of their families, at risk to alert authorities 
and the American public of grave abuses; 
Whereas, on September 14, 2020, a coalition of organizations 
filed a whistleblower complaint on behalf of Dawn 
Wooten, a nurse working at the Irwin County Detention
