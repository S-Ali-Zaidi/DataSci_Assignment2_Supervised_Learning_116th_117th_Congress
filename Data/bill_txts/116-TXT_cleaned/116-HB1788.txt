A BILL 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to limit 
the penalty for late enrollment under part B of the 
Medicare Program to 15 percent and twice the period 
of no enrollment, and to exclude periods of COBRA, 
retiree, and VA coverage from such late enrollment pen-
alty.
