A BILL 
To amend the Help America Vote Act of 2002 to establish 
election contingency plans to enable individuals to vote 
in elections for Federal office during an emergency, to 
require States to make early voting and absentee voting 
available to all individuals eligible to vote in such elec-
tions, to encourage voter registration, and for other pur-
poses.
