A BILL 
To amend the Combat Duty Pay Act of 1952 to require 
that former members of the uniformed services who were 
captured or entered a missing-in-action status during 
the Korean War while serving as a member of a combat 
unit in Korea receive combat pay for each month spent 
in a captured or missing-in-action status, rather than 
just a total of four months.
