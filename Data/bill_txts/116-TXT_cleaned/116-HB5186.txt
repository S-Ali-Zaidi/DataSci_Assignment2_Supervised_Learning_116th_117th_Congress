A BILL 
To prohibit the Secretary of the Interior from issuing new 
oil or natural gas production leases in the Gulf of Mexico 
under the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act to a person 
that does not renegotiate its existing leases in order 
to require royalty payments if oil and natural gas prices 
are greater than or equal to specified price thresholds, 
and for other purposes.
