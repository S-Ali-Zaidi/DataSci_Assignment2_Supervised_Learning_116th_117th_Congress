JOINT RESOLUTION 
Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United 
States waiving the application of the first article of 
amendment to the political speech of corporations and 
other business organizations with respect to the disburse-
ment of funds in connection with public elections and 
granting Congress and the States the power to establish 
limits on contributions and expenditures in elections for 
public office.
