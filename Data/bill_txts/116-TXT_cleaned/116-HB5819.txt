A BILL 
To amend title IV of the Employee Retirement Income Secu-
rity Act of 1974 to require the Pension Benefit Guaranty 
Corporation, in the case of airline pilots who are required 
by regulation to retire at age 60, to compute the actu-
arial value of monthly benefits in the form of a life 
annuity commencing at age 60.
