A BILL

Member of Congress from serving as a lobbyist until 
the former Member has met any obligation imposed on 
the former Member under the Congressional Account-
ability Act of 1995 to reimburse the Treasury for 
amounts paid as settlements and awards under such 
Act in the case of an act committed personally by the 
former Member.
