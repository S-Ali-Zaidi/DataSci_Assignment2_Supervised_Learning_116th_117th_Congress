A BILL 
To amend title XXVII of the Public Health Service Act, 
the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974, 
and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to prohibit a 
group health plan and a health insurance issuer offering 
group or individual health insurance coverage from re-
ducing contracted rates, or terminating contracts, with 
health care providers during a public health emergency.
