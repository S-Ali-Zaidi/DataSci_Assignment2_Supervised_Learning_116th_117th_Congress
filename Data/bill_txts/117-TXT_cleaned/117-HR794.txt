forces who conducted the airlift in Operation Allies Refuge. 
Whereas, from August 14, 2021, through August 31, 2021, 
the United States Armed Forces and coalition forces sup-
porting the United States operations in Afghanistan con-
ducted the largest non-combatant evacuation in the his-
tory of the United States Armed Forces from Hamid 
Karzai International Airport in Kabul, Afghanistan; 
Whereas United States Transportation Command led the ef-
fort, and United States Air Force Air Mobility Com-
mand, the air component, executed the operation;

Whereas 11 aeromedical evacuation flights were conducted 
from various locations to provide life-saving treatment for

Whereas on-board flight nurses and medical personnel pro-

Air Force Reserve flew missions around the clock for 17 
days;

citizens, coalition partners, and at-risk personnel each 
day;

craft were departing the airport every 34 minutes and 
evacuated more than 21,600 people in a single day;
