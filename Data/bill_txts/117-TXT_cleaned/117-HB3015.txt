A BILL 
To amend title 18, United States Code, to prohibit a Federal 
firearms licensee from selling or delivering certain semi-
automatic centerfire rifles to a person under 21 years 
of age, with exceptions for active duty military personnel 
and full-time law enforcement officers, and for other 
purposes.
