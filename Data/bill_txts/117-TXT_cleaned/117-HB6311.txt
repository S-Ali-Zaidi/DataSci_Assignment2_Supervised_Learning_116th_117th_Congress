A BILL 
To provide emergency assistance to States, territories, Tribal 
nations, and local areas affected by substance use dis-
order, including the use of opioids and stimulants, and 
to make financial assistance available to States, terri-
tories, Tribal nations, local areas, public or private non-
profit entities, and certain health providers, to provide 
for the development, organization, coordination, and op-
eration of more effective and cost efficient systems for 
the delivery of essential services to individuals with sub-
stance use disorder and their families.
