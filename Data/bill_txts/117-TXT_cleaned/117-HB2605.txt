A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
provide for a limitation on the time for the use of con-
tributions or donations by candidates for election for 
Federal office, to prohibit candidates from using cam-
paign funds to make contributions to charitable organiza-
tions which are owned or controlled by the candidate 
or immediate family members of the candidate, and for 
other purposes.
