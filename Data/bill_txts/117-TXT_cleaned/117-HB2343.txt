A BILL 
To amend the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 to 
require the Secretary of Homeland Security and the 
Commissioner of Social Security to provide information 
to States upon request which will enable States to verify 
the citizenship status of applicants for voter registration 
in elections for Federal office in the State and remove 
individuals who are not citizens of the United States 
from the list of individuals registered to vote in elections 
for Federal office in the State, and for other purposes.
