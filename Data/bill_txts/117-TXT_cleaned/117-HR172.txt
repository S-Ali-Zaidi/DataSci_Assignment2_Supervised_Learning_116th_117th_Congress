RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of February 28, as 
‘‘Desert Storm Veterans Day’’. 
Whereas Operation Desert Storm was the offensive war, part 
of the greater Persian Gulf war, that took place in Ku-
wait and Iraq; 
Whereas the main goal of Operation Desert Storm was to lib-
erate Kuwait from invading Iraqi forces and prevent the 
invasion of Saudi Arabia; 
Whereas Operation Desert Storm began on January 15, 
1991, with the deployment of United States ground, air, 
and naval forces; 
Whereas the United States and allied nations flew more than 
18,000 air deployment missions and more than 116,000 
combat air sorties;
