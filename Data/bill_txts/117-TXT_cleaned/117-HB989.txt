A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
require certain online platforms which display political 
advertisements to display with the advertisement a notice 
identifying the sponsor of the advertisement and to en-
sure that the notice will continue to be presented in 
the advertisement if a viewer of the advertisement shares 
the advertisement with others on that platform.
