RESOLUTION 
Condemning, in the strongest terms, cyberattacks carried 
out by the Government of Iran. 
Whereas, according to a Federal Bureau of Investigation 
(FBI) and Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security 
Agency (CISA) joint cybersecurity advisory, in approxi-
mately May 2021, a group of Iranian state cyber actors 
affiliated with Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps 
(IRGC), known as HomeLand Justice, gained access to 
the Government of Albania’s network; 
Whereas, in July 2022, HomeLand Justice launched a 
ransomware attack against Albania attempting to para-
lyze public services, as well as steal and destroy govern-
ment documents;
