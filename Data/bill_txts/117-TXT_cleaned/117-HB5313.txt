A BILL 
To protect children and other consumers against hazards 
associated with the accidental ingestion of button cell 
or coin batteries by requiring the Consumer Product 
Safety Commission to promulgate a consumer product 
safety standard to require child-resistant closures on con-
sumer products that use such batteries, and for other 
purposes.
