A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
prohibit certain political committees from compensating 
the spouse of the candidate for services provided to or 
on behalf of the committee, to require such committees 
to report on payments made to the spouse and the imme-
diate family members of the candidate, and for other 
purposes.
