A BILL

the Defense Production Act of 1950 to prohibit the Fed-
eral Government from limiting State access to key thera-
pies, such as monoclonal antibodies, and from prioritizing 
Federal contracts over State contracts relating to pur-
chasing supplies to combat the COVID–19 pandemic.
