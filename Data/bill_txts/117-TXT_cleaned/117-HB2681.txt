A BILL 
To amend the Inspector General Act of 1978 to require 
the Integrity Committee of the Council of the Inspectors 
General on Integrity and Efficiency to include additional 
information in requests and reports to Congress, to make 
information available to certain Members of Congress 
regarding certain allegations of wrongdoing closed with-
out referral, to require the Integrity Committee to submit 
semiannual reports to Congress and the President, to 
expand the membership of the Integrity Committee, and 
for other purposes.
