A BILL 
To require providers of broadband internet access service 
and edge services to clearly and conspicuously notify 
users of the privacy policies of those providers, to give 
users opt-in or opt-out approval rights with respect to 
the use of, disclosure of, and access to user information 
collected by those providers based on the level of sensi-
tivity of the information, and for other purposes.
