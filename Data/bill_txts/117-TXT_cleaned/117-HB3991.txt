A BILL

website or that sells or distributes a mobile application 
that maintains and stores information collected from 
such website or application in China to disclose that 
such information is stored and maintained in the Peo-
ple’s Republic of China and whether the Chinese Com-
munist Party or a Chinese state-owned entity has access 
to such information.
