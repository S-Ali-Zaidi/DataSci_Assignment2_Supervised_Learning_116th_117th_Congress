A BILL 
To amend the Pallone-Thune Telephone Robocall Abuse 
Criminal Enforcement and Deterrence Act to provide 
liability protection for the sharing of information regard-
ing suspected unlawful robocalls by or with the registered 
consortium that conducts private-led efforts to trace back 
the origin of suspected unlawful robocalls, and for the 
receipt of such information by such registered consor-
tium, and for other purposes.
