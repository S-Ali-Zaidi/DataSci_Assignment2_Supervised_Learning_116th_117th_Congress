Whereas estuary regions cover only 13 percent of the land 
area in the continental United States but contain nearly 
40 percent of the population, 39 percent of the jobs, and 
47 percent of the economic output of the United States; 
Whereas the Nation’s oceans, estuaries, and Great Lakes 
continue to fuel economic growth across the Nation with 
employment from the estuarine and ocean economy grow-
ing at 2.6 percent in 2019, compared to the national av-
erage employment growth of 1.4 percent;

Whereas, by the 1980s, the United States had already lost
