A BILL 
To amend the Public Health Service Act to direct the Sec-
retary of Health and Human Services, acting through 
the Director of the Centers for Disease Control and 
Prevention, to promote mental wellness and resilience 
and heal mental health, behavioral health, and psycho-
social problems through age and culturally appropriate 
community programs, and award grants for the purpose 
of establishing, operating, or expanding community-based 
mental wellness and resilience programs, and for other 
purposes.
