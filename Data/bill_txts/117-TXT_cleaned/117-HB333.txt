A BILL 
To amend title 10, United States Code, to permit retired 
members of the Armed Forces who have a service-con-
nected disability rated less than 50 percent to receive 
concurrent payment of both retired pay and veterans 
disability compensation, to extend eligibility for concur-
rent receipt to chapter 61 disability retirees with less 
than 20 years of service, and for other purposes.
