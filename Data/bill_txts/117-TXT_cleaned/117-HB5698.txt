A BILL 
To direct the Administrator of the Environmental Agency 
to establish a program to enhance the transparency, 
quality, and availability of life-cycle assessment data, and 
harmonize life-cycle assessment approaches to calculating 
greenhouse gas emissions and other environmental fac-
tors, in the production of products made primarily of 
eligible materials through environmental product declara-
tions or a similar mechanism as determined appropriate 
by the Administrator, and for other purposes.
