RESOLUTION 
Recognizing the Muslim holy month of Ramadan, com-
mending a month of fasting and spiritual renewal, and 
extending best wishes to Muslims in the United States 
and across the globe for a joyous and meaningful observ-
ance of Eid al-Fitr. 
Whereas Ramadan is the holy month of fasting and spiritual 
renewal for Muslims worldwide; 
Whereas this month of sacrifice and contemplation began at 
the beginning of April 2022, based on the visual 
sightings of the crescent moon; 
Whereas Muslims in the United States and around the world 
observe Ramadan by fasting, emphasizing self-discipline,
