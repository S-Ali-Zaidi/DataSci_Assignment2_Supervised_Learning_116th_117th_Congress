A BILL 
To amend title 46, United States Code, to exempt certain 
vessels transporting crude oil, petroleum, petroleum prod-
ucts, and liquified natural gas between a port in Hawaii 
and another port in the United States from Jones Act 
vessel requirements for the duration of any ban on the 
transportation of crude oil or petroleum products from 
the Russian Federation, and for other purposes.
