A BILL 
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to expand 
the requirement for States to suspend, rather than termi-
nate, an individual’s eligibility for medical assistance 
under the State Medicaid plan while the individual is 
an inmate of a public institution, to apply to inmates 
of any age.
