A BILL 
To require a report on the People’s Republic of China’s 
use of international financial institutions, to set United 
States policy regarding the identification of the People’s 
Republic of China as a ‘‘developing nation’’ and address 
this matter in regional multilateral financial institutions, 
to prohibit the use of Federal funds to purchase certain 
goods from the People’s Republic of China, and for other 
purposes.
