RESOLUTION 
Congratulating the Milwaukee Bucks, and the fans of the 
Milwaukee Bucks around the world, on winning the 2021 
National Basketball Association championship. 
Whereas, on Tuesday, July 20, 2021, the Milwaukee Bucks 
(referred to in this preamble as the ‘‘Bucks’’) won the 
2021 National Basketball Association (referred to in this 
preamble as the ‘‘NBA’’) championship by defeating the 
Phoenix Suns in Game 6 of the NBA Finals by a score 
of 105 to 98, winning the series 4 games to 2; 
Whereas the Bucks won— 
(1) the second NBA championship in the history of 
the franchise;
