A BILL 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to require 
hospitals reimbursed under the Medicare system to estab-
lish and implement security procedures to reduce the 
likelihood of infant patient abduction and baby switching, 
including procedures for identifying all infant patients 
in the hospital in a manner that ensures that it will 
be evident if infants are missing from the hospital.
