A BILL 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide 
for Medicare coverage of staff-assisted home dialysis for 
certain hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis patients and 
to ensure all patients are educated on modality options 
and receive the resources, information, and support to 
succeed on the modality of their choice, and for other 
purposes.
