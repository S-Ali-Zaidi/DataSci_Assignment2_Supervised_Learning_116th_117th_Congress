To amend the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act 
of 1977 to provide funds to States and Indian tribes 
for the purpose of promoting economic revitalization, di-
versification, and development in economically distressed 
communities through the reclamation and restoration of 
land and water resources adversely affected by coal min-
ing carried out before August 3, 1977, and for other 
purposes.
