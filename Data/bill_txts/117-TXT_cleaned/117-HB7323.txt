ify the Department of Labor’s Reentry Employment Op-
portunities grants, authorizing a competitive grant pro-
gram that aims to assist justice-involved adults and 
youth returning from incarceration with achieving aca-
demic success, employment, reducing the involvement of 
youth in crime and violence, and avoiding recidivism.
