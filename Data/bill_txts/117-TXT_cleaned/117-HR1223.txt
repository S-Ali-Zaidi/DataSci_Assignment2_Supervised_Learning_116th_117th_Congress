RESOLUTION 
Congratulating the University of Mississippi Rebels baseball 
team for winning the 2022 National Collegiate Athletic 
Association Division I baseball championship. 
Whereas, on Sunday, June 26, 2022, the University of Mis-
sissippi baseball team won the 2022 National Collegiate 
Athletic Association (referred to in this preamble as the 
‘‘NCAA’’) Men’s College World Series at Charles Schwab 
Field in Omaha, Nebraska; 
Whereas, by defeating the University of Oklahoma by a score 
of 4 to 2, the University of Mississippi baseball team be-
came the first team in school history to win the NCAA 
Division I baseball championship;
