A BILL 
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide 
equitable treatment for residents of Puerto Rico with 
respect to the refundable portion of the child tax credit 
and to provide the same treatment to families in Puerto 
Rico with one child or two children that is currently 
provided to island families with three or more children.
