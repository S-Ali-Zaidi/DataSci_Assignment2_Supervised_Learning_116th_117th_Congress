A BILL 
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to require 
coverage under State plans under the Medicaid program 
for annual lung cancer screening with no cost sharing 
for individuals for whom screening is recommended by 
U.S. Preventive Services Task Force guidelines, to ex-
pand coverage under Medicaid of counseling and 
pharmacotherapy for cessation of tobacco use, and for 
other purposes.
