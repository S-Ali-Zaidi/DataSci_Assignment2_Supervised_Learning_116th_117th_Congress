A BILL 
To amend title 36, United States Code, to require Presi-
dential Inaugural Committees to file disbursement re-
ports with the Federal Election Commission, to prohibit 
such Committees from disbursing funds for purposes un-
related to the inauguration of the President, to require 
such Committees to donate any Committee funds which 
remain available at the time the Committee terminates, 
and for other purposes.
