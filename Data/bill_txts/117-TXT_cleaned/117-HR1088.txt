RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of May 9 as ‘‘Home 
Front Heroes Day’’. 
Whereas there is not a day for acknowledging everyone on 
the home front, men and women, young and old, for their 
support, work, and sacrifices at home during the service 
of their family members, loved ones, and fellow country-
men and countrywomen in the military; 
Whereas Home Front Heroes Day is organized and spear-
headed by John Hampton ‘‘Lucky’’ Luckadoo, who 
served in the United States Army Air Corps and flew 25 
missions over Germany as a B–17 bomber pilot in World 
War II, to honor civilian Americans for their vital roles 
in supporting the military in years past and present; 
Whereas Home Front Heroes Day will recognize and cele-
