A BILL 
To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 and the Ele-
mentary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 to pro-
vide rules of construction that nothing in those Acts 
requires the use, teaching, promotion, or recommendation 
of any academic discipline, program, or activity that 
holds that the United States is a Nation founded on 
white supremacy and oppression, or that these forces 
are at the root of American society.
