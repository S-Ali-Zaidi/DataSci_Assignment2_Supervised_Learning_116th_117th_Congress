A BILL 
To require the Government Accountability Office to evaluate 
the effects of anticompetitive contracting clauses in con-
tracts between health insurers and health care providers 
and to determine actions taken by the Federal Trade 
Commission and the Department of Justice relating to 
the use of such clauses in such contracts and to assess 
their ability to effectively enforce the Federal antitrust 
laws with respect to such use.
