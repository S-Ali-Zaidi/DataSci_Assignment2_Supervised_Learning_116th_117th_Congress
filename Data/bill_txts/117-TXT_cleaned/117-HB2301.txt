A BILL 
To amend the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 to 
require each State to ensure that each individual who 
provides identifying information to the State motor vehi-
cle authority is automatically registered to vote in elec-
tions for Federal office held in the State unless the 
individual does not meet the eligibility requirements for 
registering to vote in such elections or declines to be 
registered to vote in such elections, and for other pur-
poses.
