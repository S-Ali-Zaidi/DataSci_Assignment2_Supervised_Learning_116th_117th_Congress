A BILL 
To amend title 10, United States Code, to eliminate the 
recoupment of separation pay, special separation benefits, 
and voluntary separation incentive payments from mem-
bers of the Armed Forces who subsequently receive dis-
ability compensation under laws administered by the De-
partment of Veterans Affairs and to impose limitations 
on the authority of the Secretary of Defense to recoup 
such pay from members who subsequently receive mili-
tary retired or retainer pay.
