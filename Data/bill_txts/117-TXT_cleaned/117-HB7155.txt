A BILL 
To prohibit the importation of petroleum and petroleum 
products from the Russian Federation, to amend the 
Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to establish an income 
tax credit for the sale or blending of certain fuels con-
taining ethanol and to extend tax incentives for biodiesel 
and renewable diesel, to amend the Clean Air Act with 
respect to the ethanol waiver for Reid vapor pressure 
limitations under that Act, and for other purposes.
