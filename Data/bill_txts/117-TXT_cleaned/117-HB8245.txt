A BILL 
To require the Secretary of Health and Human Services 
to award grants to support community-based coverage 
entities to carry out a comprehensive coverage program 
that provides to qualifying individuals and small busi-
nesses health coverage and integrated social determinant 
of health support services to small business workers that 
promote improved health, long-term economic self-suffi-
ciency, employment and retention, and for other pur-
poses.
