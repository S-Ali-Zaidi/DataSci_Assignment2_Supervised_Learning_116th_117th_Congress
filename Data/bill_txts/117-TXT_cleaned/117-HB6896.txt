A BILL 
To amend the Controlled Substances Act to improve con-
sumer take-back programs by allowing persons who are 
authorized to collect controlled substances from ultimate 
users and other non-registrants for destruction through 
an authorized consumer return program to open and 
inspect packages, to provide a secure method of trans-
porting substances to another location of destruction, 
and for other purposes.
