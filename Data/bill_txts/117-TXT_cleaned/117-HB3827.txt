A BILL 
To amend section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934 
to ensure that the immunity under such section 
incentivizes online platforms to responsibly address illegal 
content while not immunizing the disparate treatment 
of ideological viewpoints and continuing to encourage 
a vibrant, open, and competitive internet, and for other 
purposes.
