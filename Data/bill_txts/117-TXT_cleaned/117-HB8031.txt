A BILL 
To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to 
prohibit certain political committees from compensating 
an immediate family member of the candidate for serv-
ices provided to or on behalf of the committee, to require 
such committees to report on payments made to imme-
diate family members of the candidate, and for other 
purposes.
