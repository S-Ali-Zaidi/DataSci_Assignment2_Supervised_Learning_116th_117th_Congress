CONCURRENT RESOLUTION 
Expressing the sense of Congress that August 30, 2021, 
be observed as the 131st anniversary of the 1890 Institu-
tions. 
Whereas former Senator Justin Morrill helped lead the pas-
sage of the Second Morrill Act of 1890 on August 30, 
1890 (7 U.S.C. 321 et seq.), to establish and support 
land-grant institutions for African Americans to attend; 
Whereas the mission of the 1890 Institutions (as defined in 
section 2 of the Agricultural Research, Extension, and 
Education Reform Act of 1998 (7 U.S.C. 7601)) is edu-
cational instruction, agricultural research, and statewide 
extension efforts;

Whereas collectively, these institutions have over 88,000 un-

(1) agriculture and related sciences;

across the 1890 Institutions;
