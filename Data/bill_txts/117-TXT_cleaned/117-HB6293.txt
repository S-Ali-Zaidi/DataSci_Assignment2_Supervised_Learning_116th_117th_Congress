A BILL 
To amend the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 to 
require States to designate public high schools as voter 
registration agencies, to direct such schools to conduct 
voter registration drives for students attending such 
schools, to direct the Secretary of Education to make 
grants to reimburse such schools for the costs of con-
ducting such voter registration drives, and for other pur-
poses.
