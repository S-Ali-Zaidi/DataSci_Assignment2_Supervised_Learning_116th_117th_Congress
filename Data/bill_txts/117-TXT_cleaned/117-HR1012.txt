RESOLUTION 
Congratulating Gregg Popovich, Head Coach of the San An-
tonio Spurs, on becoming the winningest head coach 
in the history of the National Basketball Association. 
Whereas, on March 11, 2022, Gregg Popovich earned his 
record setting 1,336th win as a head coach when the San 
Antonio Spurs defeated the Utah Jazz, 104 to 102; 
Whereas Coach Gregg Popovich has led the San Antonio 
Spurs as Head Coach for 26 seasons; 
Whereas, over 26 seasons, Coach Popovich has led the San 
Antonio Spurs to five National Basketball Association 
championships in 1999, 2003, 2005, 2007, and 2014; 
Whereas, over 26 seasons, Coach Popovich has led the San 
Antonio Spurs to six Western Conference Championships 
in 1999, 2003, 2005, 2007, 2013, and 2014;
