A BILL 
To amend the Employee Retirement Income Security Act 
of 1974, title XXVII of the Public Health Service Act, 
and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to require group 
health plans and health insurance issuers offering group 
or individual health insurance coverage to provide for 
3 primary care visits and 3 behavioral health care visits 
without application of any cost-sharing requirement.
