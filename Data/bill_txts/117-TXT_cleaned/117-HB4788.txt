A BILL 
To amend title II of the Social Security Act to restrict 
the application of the windfall elimination provision to 
individuals whose combined monthly income from bene-
fits under such title and other monthly periodic payments 
exceeds a minimum wage-indexed amount of $5,500 and 
to provide for a graduated implementation of such provi-
sion on amounts above such minimum amount.
