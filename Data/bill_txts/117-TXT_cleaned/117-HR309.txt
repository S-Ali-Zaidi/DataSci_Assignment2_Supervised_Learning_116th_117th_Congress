RESOLUTION 
To amend the Rules of the House of Representatives to 
prohibit bills and joint resolutions that contain an appro-
priation or authorization of more than $100,000,000,000 
relating to infrastructure that does not provide at least 
50 percent of such funds to programs, initiatives, or 
projects that directly impact construction or maintenance 
of highways, roads, bridges, dams, passenger and freight 
railways, airports, waterways and ports, public schools, 
or the safety of such facilities.
