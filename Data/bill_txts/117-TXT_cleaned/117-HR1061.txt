Supporting the goals and ideals of National Deaf History 
Month. 
Whereas the purpose of National Deaf History Month is to 
increase awareness of and express appreciation for the 
many contributions that deaf and hard-of-hearing individ-
uals have made to our society; 
Whereas, according to 2017 Census Bureau estimates, at 
least 11,200,000 individuals in the United States are 
deaf or hard of hearing; 
Whereas deaf culture is an important part of the fabric of 
our society; 
Whereas deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals have made and 
continue to make numerous contributions to the United 
States;
