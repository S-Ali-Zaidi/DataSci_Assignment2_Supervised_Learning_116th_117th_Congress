cialist Bishop E. Evans.

was born in Arlington, Texas, raised by his grandmother, 
and attended Mansfield High School, where he was a 
member of the Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps; 
Whereas SPC Evans enlisted in the Texas Army National

Field Artillery Regiment in New Braunfels, Texas;
