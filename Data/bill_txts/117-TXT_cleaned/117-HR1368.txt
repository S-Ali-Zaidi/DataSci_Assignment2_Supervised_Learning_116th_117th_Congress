Supporting the idea of celebrating the 50th year of the 
Walnut Valley Festival. 
Whereas Bob and Kendra Redford, Stuart Mossman, and Joe 
Muret founded the Walnut Valley Festival in the Fourth 
District of Kansas back in 1972; 
Whereas Bob Redford served more than 40 years as its presi-
dent until his passing in December 2016; 
Whereas Bob and Kendra Redford’s son Bart is the Execu-
tive Director of the Walnut Valley Festival; 
Whereas Bob and Kendra Redford, Stuart Mossman and Joe 
Muret’s dedication to live music resulted in this leg-
endary event, which is one of the longest running music 
festivals in United States history, and for 50 years, pick-
