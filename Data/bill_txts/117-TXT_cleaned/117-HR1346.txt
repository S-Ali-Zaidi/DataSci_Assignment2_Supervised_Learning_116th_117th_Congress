RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of the week beginning 
on September 11, 2022, as ‘‘Celebrate Community Week’’. 
Whereas ‘‘Celebrate Community’’ is a weeklong initiative to 
promote joint community service among members of 
Lions Clubs International, Rotary International, Kiwanis 
International, and Optimist International, and the pur-
pose is to elevate the collective social impact of the orga-
nizations and promote a spirit of worldwide volunteer col-
laboration; 
Whereas, from September 11 through September 17, thou-
sands of local area Kiwanis, Lions, Optimist, and Rotary
