A BILL 
To allow Americans to receive paid leave time to process 
and address their own health needs and the health needs 
of their partners during the period following a pregnancy 
loss, an unsuccessful round of intrauterine insemination 
or of an assisted reproductive technology procedure, a 
failed adoption arrangement, a failed surrogacy arrange-
ment, or a diagnosis or event that impacts pregnancy 
or fertility, to support related research and education, 
and for other purposes.
