A BILL 
To amend the Migratory Bird Treaty Act to affirm that 
the Migratory Bird Treaty Act’s prohibition on the unau-
thorized take or killing of migratory birds includes inci-
dental take by commercial activities, and to direct the 
United States Fish and Wildlife Service to regulate such 
incidental take, and for other purposes.
