A BILL 
To amend the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act to provide 
a timetable for the collection of medical debt by debt 
collectors, to amend the Fair Credit Reporting Act to 
prohibit consumer reporting agencies from issuing con-
sumer reports containing information about debts related 
to medically necessary procedures, and for other pur-
poses.
