A BILL 
To deter, prevent, reduce, and respond to harassment in 
the workplace, including sexual harassment, sexual as-
sault, and harassment based on protected categories; and 
to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to modify 
the tax treatment of amounts related to employment 
discrimination and harassment in the workplace, includ-
ing sexual harassment, sexual assault, and harassment 
based on protected categories.
