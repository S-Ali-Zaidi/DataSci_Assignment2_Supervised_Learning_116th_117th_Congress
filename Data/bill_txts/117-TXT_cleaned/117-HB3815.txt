A BILL 
To permit legally married same-sex couples to amend their 
filing status for income tax returns outside the statute 
of limitations, to amend the Internal Revenue Code of 
1986 to clarify that all provisions shall apply to legally 
married same-sex couples in the same manner as other 
married couples, and for other purposes.
