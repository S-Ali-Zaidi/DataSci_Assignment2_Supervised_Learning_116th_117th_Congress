A BILL 
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to eliminate 
cost-sharing with respect to coverage of insulin as a 
covered part D drug and associated medical supplies 
and to provide mail order access to insulin and such 
supplies under the Medicare program during the 
COVID–19 emergency period.
