a methodology for determining State allotments for Med-
icaid disproportionate share hospital payments that is 
based on State poverty levels, to require States to 
prioritize disproportionate share hospital payments on 
the basis of Medicaid inpatient utilization and low-income 
utilization rates, and for other purposes.
