RESOLUTION 
Recognizing the 46th anniversary of the Fall of Saigon on 
April 30, 1975. 
Whereas since 1975, hundreds of thousands of Vietnamese 
have resettled in the United States and built vibrant 
communities across the United States which contribute to 
the rich diversity of the Nation; 
Whereas, on April 30, 1975, the Republic of Vietnam was 
overthrown by communist forces in an event known as 
‘‘Black April’’ or the ‘‘Fall of Saigon’’, which is observed 
in Vietnamese-American communities across the United 
States each year;
