RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of February 19 
through February 26, 2022, as ‘‘National FFA Week’’, 
recognizing the important role of the National Future 
Farmers of America (referred to in this resolution as 
‘‘FFA’’) Organization in developing the next generation 
of leaders who will change the world, and celebrating 
70 years of the National FFA Organization’s member-
ship magazine, The National Future Farmer, now called 
New Horizons. 
Whereas the mission of the Future Farmers of America (re-
ferred to in this resolution as the ‘‘FFA’’) is to make a 
positive difference in the lives of students by developing 
their potential for premier leadership, personal growth, 
and career success through agricultural education; 
Whereas the National FFA Organization was established in 
1928; 
Whereas there are 735,038 FFA members, aged 12 to 21, in 
8,817 chapters in all 50 States, the District of Columbia, 
Puerto Rico, and the Virgin Islands; 
Whereas the National FFA Organization welcomes all stu-
dents; 
Whereas more than 13,000 FFA advisors and agricultural 
education teachers deliver an integrated model of agricul-
tural education, providing students with innovative and 
leading-edge education; 
Whereas 2022 marks 70 years of the National FFA Organi-
zation’s membership-magazine, The National Future 
Farmer, now called New Horizons;
