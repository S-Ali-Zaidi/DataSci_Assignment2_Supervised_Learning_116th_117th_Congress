A BILL 
To amend the Act of August 16, 1937 (commonly referred 
to as the ‘‘National Apprenticeship Act’’) and expand 
the national apprenticeship system to include apprentice-
ships, youth apprenticeships, and pre-apprenticeship reg-
istered under such Act, to promote the furtherance of 
labor standards necessary to safeguard the welfare of 
apprentices, and for other purposes.
